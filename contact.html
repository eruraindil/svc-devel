the volunteer outlet

Stop by our home in Raithby House, by the Cannon, Old Jeramiah.

if we miss you

Sometimes the staff run programs during our office hours and we have to close the office. We try to leave information on our front door showing where we are, or when we will be back. We're sorry for missing you, please send us an email and we will be happy to make an appointment.

accessibility♿

The Raithby House front door is mobility device accessible, and the SVC office can be reached by going through the Student Support Network office, to the back of the house.
