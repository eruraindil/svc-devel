welcome

As Canada's first & only incorporated student run volunteer centre, Student Volunteer Connections works to ignite, educate, & foster involvement of University of Guelph students through the world of volunteerism. Student Volunteer Connections strives to promote positive social action for the purpose of building skills, passions, and most of all, community.

Student Volunteer Connections is proud to present this University of Guelph portal to accessing involvement opportunities, resources, and a network of 300 volunteer-based organizations.
